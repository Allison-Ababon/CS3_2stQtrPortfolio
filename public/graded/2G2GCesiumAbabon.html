Chococat

This is Chococat, an amazing and underrated sanrio character that I really luv! As you can see, he has beautiful luxurious chocolate-like fur (hence the name..) and a cute blue collar. He is obviously very loveable and I think would make a pretty awesomesauce pet. Just from a single glance of his stance, he looks like he would make a very modest gentleman-ish of a cat.

As mentioned earlier, Chococat is unfortunately underrated and is often overlooked by most Sanrio lovers. This is definitely undeserved since I think that he is just as cute as the rest of the characters. His minimalistic design makes him especially endearing!
